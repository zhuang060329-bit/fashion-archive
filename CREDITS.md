## Factual Confidence Levels

This archive uses three confidence levels, documented in `src/data/types.ts`:

- **verified** — claim supported by official brand records, contemporaneous press, or academic sources
- **widely-reported** — claim appears consistently in multiple credible sources; not from a single origin
- **contested** — claim is disputed or varies across sources; noted in source notes
